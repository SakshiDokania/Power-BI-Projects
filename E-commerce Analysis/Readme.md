# Ecommerce Analytics in Power BI — Case Study

An end-to-end Power BI case study analyzing ecommerce data for **Whiskique**, an online pet supply company. The project covers the full analytics workflow: from data modeling and cleaning to DAX-powered metrics and executive-level dashboards that answer real business questions around sales, customers, and shipping costs.


## Project Overview

The Whiskique executive team needed clear visibility into how their sales are performing, where their customers are located, and how to reduce shipping expenses. This project tackles those challenges using Power BI — combining multiple data sources, building meaningful metrics, and presenting findings through interactive dashboard-style report pages.

**Key business questions answered:**
- How are sales and profits performing across products and regions?
- Who are the most profitable customers?
- Which products are frequently purchased together?
- What is the impact of shipping higher quantities on shipping costs?
- Where can shipping expenses be reduced?


##  What's Inside

### Data Exploration
- Combined multiple files and built a data model for sales and expense analysis
- Used **Power Query** to clean data and filter out invalid records
- Created **new customer measures** and analyzed order metrics
- Explored products and shipping quantities
- Built a **market basket visualization** to identify frequently bought-together products

### Ecommerce Analytics
- Identified the most **profitable products and customers**
- Built a **sales by state map** using state-region mapping
- Analyzed **shipping costs by product**
- Created **What-If analysis** metrics (Parts 1 & 2) to model the impact of shipping larger quantities on costs

### Visualize Your Analysis
- Designed cohesive **dashboard-style report pages** for the executive team
- Built **revenue and profit** visuals and an executive summary
- Created a **profit percentage treemap** for product-level breakdown
- Finalized a **shipping costs dashboard** to support cost-reduction decisions
- Delivered actionable recommendations on reducing shipping expenses
