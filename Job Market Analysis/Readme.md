#  Job Market Analysis in Power BI — Case Study

An end-to-end Power BI case study analyzing job posting data for **DataSearch**, a fictional recruitment company. The project uncovers the most in-demand skills for data scientists, data analysts, and data engineers — combining Power Query data cleaning, DAX-powered trend analysis, and an interactive business dashboard delivered to the DataSearch team.

---

##  Project Overview

DataSearch needed to understand what skills are driving the job market for data roles, how salary expectations vary with experience, and what trends are emerging across job postings. This project tackles those questions — importing and cleaning raw job posting data, extracting key market insights, and building a polished interactive dashboard to present findings.

**Key business questions answered:**
- What skills are most in-demand for data scientists, analysts, and engineers?
- How does years of experience impact salary?
- What trends are emerging in job postings over time?
- Which skills are growing or declining in demand?
- What job recommendations can be made based on skill profiles?

##  What's Inside

### Exploratory Analysis
- Imported and cleaned the job postings dataset using **Power Query**
- Defined key **EDA questions** to guide the analysis
- Performed **job posting trend analysis** across roles
- Cleaned and standardized **job titles** for consistent grouping
- Analyzed the **effect of years of experience on salary**
- Summarized initial **trends found through EDA**

### Analyzing Market Trends
- Identified **key insights** to focus on for the DataSearch team
- Performed **skill cleanup and analysis** across job descriptions
- Calculated the **likelihood of skills appearing** in job postings
- Tracked **trends in skills over time** to spot growing demand
- Conducted a **deep dive into key job descriptions**
- Generated **job recommendations** based on skill alignment
- Summarized all **trends from the analysis**

### Dashboard Build
- Designed and built an **interactive job market dashboard** for DataSearch
- Created **dashboard templates** and finalized the layout
- Built visuals for **top skills by job role**
- Added a **salary gauge** for company-level benchmarking
- Incorporated **bookmarks for interactivity** and smooth navigation
- Delivered a complete, presentation-ready final dashboard
